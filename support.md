## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will my photos actually be deleted?

When you swipe left, the photo is **marked** for deletion but not removed yet. When you tap the red trash button at the top of the screen, iOS shows its own confirmation dialog — only after you tap "Delete" there are the photos actually removed. They go to the **Recently Deleted** album in Apple's Photos app for 30 days, where you can recover anything by mistake.

### How do I undo a swipe?

Tap the curved arrow button between the X and ❤️. It restores the previous swipe instantly.

### I bought Pro but it's not active

Tap the trash pill or hit the daily limit screen → **Restore Purchases**. This re-syncs with your Apple ID. If that doesn't work:

1. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID that purchased Pro
2. Pull down to refresh, or kill and relaunch the app
3. If still stuck, email vilgot@moeschlin.com with your Apple ID region and the date of purchase

### Will Pro carry over to a new iPhone?

Yes. Sign into the same Apple ID on the new device, install the app, and Pro is automatically restored. You never pay twice.

### What happens to my Pro purchase if I delete the app?

Nothing. The purchase is permanently linked to your Apple ID. Reinstall any time and Pro is back.

### Why does PhotoSwipe need photo access?

To show your photos as swipe cards and to ask iOS to delete the ones you mark. We never copy, upload, or transmit any photo or video. See the [privacy policy](/privacy/).

### Can I get a refund?

In-app purchases on the App Store are processed by Apple, not us. To request a refund, visit <https://reportaproblem.apple.com> and select your PhotoSwipe Pro purchase. Apple decides on refunds; we have no say in that decision.

### I have a feature request / bug

Email vilgot@moeschlin.com. Be specific (iOS version, what you did, what happened) and I'll get back to you.
