# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 29 April 2026
**Last updated:** 29 April 2026

## Summary, in one sentence

PhotoSwipe runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data — because we have no servers and no analytics.

---

## 1. Who we are

PhotoSwipe ("the app", "we", "us", "our") is published by Vilgot Moeschlin, an individual developer based in Sweden, in the role of data controller under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

**Contact:** vilgot@moeschlin.com

## 2. What data the app accesses on your device

PhotoSwipe needs access to your **Photos library** to function. With your explicit permission (granted via the system prompt at first launch, as required by Apple iOS), the app:

- Reads photos and videos to display them as swipe cards
- Reads metadata (date, dimensions, file size, duration) to show on each card
- Submits a deletion request to iOS when you confirm a batch delete

All of this happens locally on your device. **Your photos, videos, and any data derived from them never leave your device, never reach our servers, and are never seen by us.**

## 3. What data we receive when you purchase Pro

When you purchase "PhotoSwipe Pro" as an in-app purchase, **Apple processes the transaction as the App Store payment processor**. We do not see your payment details, your name, or your Apple ID.

Apple provides us only with aggregated, anonymised sales reports (for example: "10 units sold in Sweden this week"). These reports cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's own privacy policy: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>

## 4. Data we do not collect

To be explicit, PhotoSwipe does **not** collect:

- Your name, email, phone number, or address
- Your IP address or device identifiers
- Crash logs or analytics
- Photo content, metadata, or deletion history
- Browsing or usage patterns

We use no third-party SDKs that collect data. There are no cookies, trackers, or advertising networks.

## 5. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Article 6)

To the extent any processing occurs:

| Activity | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Accessing your photo library after you grant permission | Consent — Article 6(1)(a) |
| Receiving anonymised IAP sales reports from Apple | Performance of a contract — Article 6(1)(b) |

## 6. Data retention

We retain no personal data because we collect none. Apple's standard transaction record retention applies to your in-app purchase and is governed by Apple's policies.

## 7. Your rights under the GDPR

As a person whose data is governed by the GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** any personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** any inaccuracies
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** or **object to** processing
- **Data portability**
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (revoke photo access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → PhotoSwipe)
- **Lodge a complaint** with the Swedish supervisory authority, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY), at <https://www.imy.se>

Because we hold no personal data, in practice there is nothing for us to access, rectify, or erase. To exercise any right, contact vilgot@moeschlin.com and we will respond within 30 days.

## 8. International data transfers

We do not transfer any personal data internationally because we do not collect any. Apple's processing of your in-app purchase may involve transfers governed by Apple's own contractual safeguards.

## 9. Children

PhotoSwipe is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact vilgot@moeschlin.com.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the **Effective date** at the top and, where appropriate, notify users in the next app update.
